INTRODUCTION

This project contains all of the datasets in use at Democracy Unchained. The
databases contain information such as the structure of the US electoral
college, population demographics, voting history in elections, and other
data related to democracy and election processes.

This data is compatible with our microservices architecture, which may
be found here: https://github.com/DemocracyUnchained/microservices
